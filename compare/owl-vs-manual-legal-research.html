When manual research still makes sense

Highly novel questions with no analog precedent, jurisdiction-specific trial-court quirks, or matters requiring sworn investigator work may need human-led research beyond any automation. OWL is strongest on structured case-law analysis, precedent mapping, and first-draft memos where public legal databases apply.
