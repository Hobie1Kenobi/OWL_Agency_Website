How to Ask the Right Question

A strong research request includes:

  • Issue: The specific legal question (e.g., "Was the warrantless CSLI search lawful?")
  • Jurisdiction: Court and circuit (e.g., "U.S. Supreme Court / Sixth Circuit on cert")
  • Facts: Key undisputed facts affecting the analysis
  • Relief: What your client needs (suppression, summary judgment, etc.)
