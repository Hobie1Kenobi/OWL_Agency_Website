Frequently Asked Questions

Does accepting XRP expose my firm to cryptocurrency volatility?

Not necessarily. Most legal payment processors offer automatic conversion to your preferred fiat currency at the moment of transaction, eliminating volatility concerns completely. This "accept crypto, receive dollars" approach provides all the benefits without the risks.

What about regulatory compliance?

The 2025 XRP Legal Compliance Suite addresses regulatory requirements specifically for law firms. Features include built-in KYC/AML verification, IOLTA-compatible trust accounting, automated tax reporting, and documentation that satisfies bar association requirements in 42 jurisdictions.

How do clients without XRP make payments?

Modern payment processors provide client-facing tools that allow payment by credit card, bank transfer, or other traditional methods that are then converted to XRP behind the scenes. This hybrid approach ensures firms can benefit from XRP efficiencies without requiring clients to hold cryptocurrency.
